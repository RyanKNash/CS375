EXTREME CAPUCHIN FACT ZONE

  • Capuchins are New World monkeys from Central and South America.
  • They are extremely intelligent and can use tools.
  • They eat fruit, insects, small animals, seeds, and basically whatever looks interesting.
  • They live in social groups and communicate with calls, faces, and body movement.
  • They are named after Capuchin friars because their head coloring looks like a hood.

Capuchins are proof that being small does not stop you from causing maximum chaos.
